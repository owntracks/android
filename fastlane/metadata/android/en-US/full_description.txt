<p><i>OwnTracks</i> allows you to keep track of your own location. You can build your private location diary or share it with your family and friends. <i>OwnTracks</i> is open-source and uses open protocols for communication so you can be sure your data stays secure and private.</p><p>For more information see <a href='https://owntracks.org/booklet' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>our documentation</a>.</p>
